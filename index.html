Making an Audio-Visual presentation

Introduction

For my final project I chose to focus on a class that I had conducted for a 2ESO class, teaching them how to conduct an Audio-visual presentation. After seeing the mistakes that the 2ESO class had made in their powerpoint presentations I was asked if I could conduct a lessons specifically on how to present their topics.

This lesson was used over a range of year levels as well as over both History & Geography classes, as well as English classes. It was original in that in order to teach them how to make a presentation, I had to also conduct a presentation that abided by what I was teaching - thus providing an example while showing what was required.

What subject/s Was This Lesson Useful In?

The topic of presentation and public speaking is useful most subjects, but most importantly in the field of the humanities. Presenting ideas is a skill that crosses disciplines.

What Topic Was I Teaching?

I was teaching the students how to make an engaging and visually appealing presentation. I observed the students make their own presentations previously, so I was able to identify areas where they could improve their presentation skills.

What Was My Objective?

My objective was for the students to get the fundamentals right. To do this I outlined exactly what they needed to do to make a fundamentally engaging presentation.

How Did Me and The Teacher Compliment Each Other?

After the presentation both me and the teacher fielded questions from the students, clarifying areas that were not clear, and expanding on areas the students wanted more information on.

What Activity Were the Students tasked With Achieving?

The students were then tasked with the goal of creating a presentation about either a country of their choice or one that they made up. This served two purposes:

  • They could practice the knowledged passed onto them in the lesson
  • We could evaluate the students understanding.
